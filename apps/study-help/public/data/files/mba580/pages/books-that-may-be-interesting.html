Books that may be interesting

Books that May be Interesting:

  • HBRs 10 Must reads on Strategy
    • Prof. Gubler Comments: ten HBR articles that are highly read, highly distributed and highly respected
    • ISBN-10: 1422157989
  • Good Strategy Bad Strategy: The Difference and Why It Matters by Richard Rumelt
    • Prof. Gubler Comments: a very nice discussion of "good" and "bad" strategy and how you can recognize bad strategy. Overview of strategy fundamentals.
    • ISBN-10: 0307886239
  • The Art of Diagnosis by Jackson Nickerson
    • Prof. Gubler Comments: excellent pamphlet on critical thinking, thinking biases, and diagnosing and formulating problems.
    • ISBN: 9781696079136
  • Leading Change from the Middle by Jackson Nickerson
    • Prof. Gubler Comments: another excellent book on leadership and critical thinking.
    • ISBN: 9780815725220
  • Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman
    • A very popular book on critical thinking and decision-making.
  • Blue Ocean Strategy: How to Create Uncontested Market Space and Make Competition Irrelevant by Kim and Mauborgne
    • Prof. Gubler comments: a nice book to help you see how to find uncontested market spaces where you can compete effectively (e.g. looking for local monopolies)
    • ISBN-10: 1591396190
  • Contemporary Strategy Analysis: Concepts, Techniques and Applications (textbook)
    • Prof. Gubler Comments: Grant produces a very nice textbook, and he has been producing it for many, many years.  This means that you can get an earlier edition for pretty cheap.  The core content of strategy has not changed dramatically for the last 15-20 years.
    • ISBN 10: 0631231366
  • Strategic Management by Dyer, Godfrey, Jensen and Bryce (textbook)
    • Prof. Gubler comments: this is a good textbook written by my colleagues here at BYU. One of the better textbooks I have seen. Great video animations supplement the textbook material.
    • ISBN-10: 0470937386
  • Strategic Mangement by Rothaermel (textbook)
    • Prof. Gubler Comments: One of the best strategy textbooks. Very complete and carefully done.  It is a pricey book.  My recommendation would be to buy it used if you can, or, even better, buy an old edition of Grant (above) for $3.00.
    • ISBN-10: 1259420477
