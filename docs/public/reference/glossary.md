# Glossary

Key terms and concepts used in JD Agent.

---

## A

### Action
A single, physical next step that can be done. See also: [Next Action](#next-action).

### Agent
The AI assistant in JD Agent that can help manage tasks, calendar, and vault through natural conversation.

### Archive
1. Where completed tasks are stored permanently in the Vault
2. The "A" in PARA - inactive/completed projects and reference

### Area
An ongoing sphere of responsibility without a deadline. Examples: Health, Career, Family. The "A" in PARA.

---

## B

### Backlog
A list of known issues, planned enhancements, and feature requests. See [Backlog](/docs/roadmap/backlog.md).

### Brain Dump
A quick capture session where you get everything out of your head into the inbox.

---

## C

### Capture
The first step in GTD: collecting thoughts, tasks, and ideas into your inbox without organizing.

### Ceremony
An automated briefing sent at scheduled times (morning, evening, weekly).

### Clarify
The second step in GTD: processing inbox items to decide if they're actionable and what the next step is.

### Context
A tag indicating WHERE or HOW you can do a task (e.g., @computer, @calls, @errands).

### Command Center
The main dashboard app for JD Agent (http://localhost:5173).

---

## D

### Daily Review
A quick daily check of your Today view and inbox. Part of the GTD Reflect step.

### Due Date
The deadline by which a task MUST be completed. Different from [Scheduled Date](#scheduled-date).

---

## E

### Energy Level
A task property indicating the mental/physical energy required: high, low, or admin.

### Engage
The fifth step in GTD: actually doing the work, choosing tasks based on context, time, and energy.

---

## G

### GTD (Getting Things Done)
A personal productivity methodology by David Allen. The five steps: Capture, Clarify, Organize, Reflect, Engage.

---

## H

### Hub
The backend API server for JD Agent (http://localhost:3000).

### Hard Deadline
A due date that cannot be moved (e.g., tax filing deadline).

---

## I

### Inbox
The collection point for all captured items before processing. Goal is "inbox zero" - processed, not necessarily done.

### Inbox Zero
The state of having processed all items in your inbox. Items are moved, scheduled, or deleted - not necessarily completed.

### Integration
A connection between JD Agent and an external service (e.g., Google Calendar, Telegram).

---

## J

### Journal
Daily entries in the Vault for reflection and planning.

---

## L

### Label
A tag for cross-cutting categorization (e.g., #urgent, #client-x). Different from [Context](#context).

---

## N

### Next Action
The very next physical, visible activity needed to move something forward. A core GTD concept.

---

## O

### Organize
The third step in GTD: putting clarified items in the right place (projects, calendar, contexts, waiting for, someday/maybe).

---

## P

### PARA
A knowledge organization system by Tiago Forte: Projects, Areas, Resources, Archive.

### Priority
A task's urgency level: P1 (urgent), P2 (high), P3 (medium), P4 (low).

### Project
1. In GTD: any outcome requiring more than one action step
2. In PARA: an active outcome with a deadline

---

## Q

### Quick Add
The task creation feature that understands natural language. Access via `Q` key or the input bar.

---

## R

### Reflect
The fourth step in GTD: reviewing your system regularly (daily review, weekly review).

### Resources
Reference materials organized by topic. The "R" in PARA.

### Recurring Task
A task that repeats on a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.).

---

## S

### Scheduled Date
When you plan to WORK on a task. Different from [Due Date](#due-date).

### Section
A subdivision within a project for organizing tasks (similar to Todoist sections).

### Semantic Search
Finding content by meaning rather than exact keywords. Uses AI embeddings.

### Someday/Maybe
A GTD list for ideas you might want to do eventually but not now.

### Status
A task's current state: inbox, today, upcoming, waiting, someday, done, archived.

### Subtask
A task nested under another task, representing a step within a larger task.

---

## T

### Task
A single action item to be completed.

### Time Block
A scheduled period on your calendar dedicated to specific work.

### Time Estimate
Expected duration for a task (e.g., ~30m, ~2h).

### Tool
A capability available to the AI agent (e.g., task_create, calendar_today).

---

## V

### Vault
JD Agent's knowledge management system - a Notion-like repository for notes, documents, and archives.

---

## W

### Waiting For
A task status indicating you're waiting on someone else before you can proceed.

### Weekly Review
A comprehensive GTD review done weekly to process inboxes, review projects, and plan ahead.

---
