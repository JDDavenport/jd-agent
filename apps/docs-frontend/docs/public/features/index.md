# Features Overview

JD Agent provides a comprehensive suite of productivity tools built on GTD principles.

---

## Core Features

### [Task Management](./tasks/index.md)
Your command center for getting things done.
- Inbox for capturing
- Today and Upcoming views
- Projects with sections
- Contexts and labels
- Recurring tasks
- Natural language quick add

### [Vault (Knowledge Base)](./vault/index.md)
Your second brain for storing and finding information.
- Notes and documents
- Full-text and semantic search
- Daily journal
- Task archive
- File attachments

### [Calendar](./calendar/index.md)
Keep track of your schedule.
- Google Calendar sync
- Event management
- Conflict detection
- Time blocking

### [Budget & Finance](./finance/index.md)
Monitor spending and manage category budgets.
- Plaid Link (Chase supported)
- Budget tracking with remaining spend
- Email/SMS/Telegram alerts

### [AI Agent](./agent/index.md)
Your intelligent assistant.
- 37 specialized tools
- Natural language commands
- Smart classification
- Available via web and Telegram

### [Personal Health](./health/index.md)
Track your fitness and wellness metrics.
- Whoop recovery scores
- Sleep quality tracking
- Health status dashboard
- Recovery and readiness insights

### [Ceremonies](./ceremonies/index.md)
Automated check-ins to keep you on track.
- Morning briefings
- Evening reviews
- Weekly planning

### [Goals & Habits](./goals/index.md)
Track goals across life areas and build lasting habits.
- 6 life areas (Spiritual, Personal, Fitness, Family, Professional, School)
- Goal health scoring and milestones
- Habit tracking with streaks
- Progress dashboard and reflections

### [Daily Journal](./daily-journal/index.md)
Evening review workflow to reflect on the day.
- 7-step guided review process
- Habit completion tracking during review
- Rich text journal with auto-save
- Tomorrow preview and preparation
- Mood and tag tracking
- Vault integration for permanent records

### [Integrations](./integrations/index.md)
Connect with your existing tools.
- Google Calendar & Gmail
- Canvas LMS
- Telegram
- Notion, Apple Notes
- Whoop

---

## Specialized Agents

### [Job Hunting Agent](./job-hunting/index.md)
AI-powered job search automation.
- 31 specialized tools
- Intelligent job matching and scoring
- Resume variant selection
- Cover letter generation
- Browser automation (LinkedIn, MBA Exchange)
- Application tracking pipeline
- Interview management

### [Testing Agent](./testing-agent/index.md)
Autonomous AI-powered testing using GPT-4 Vision.
- 18 testing tools
- Visual UI analysis
- Automatic exploration and testing
- Bug detection and reporting
- HTML/JSON test reports
- Playwright browser automation
