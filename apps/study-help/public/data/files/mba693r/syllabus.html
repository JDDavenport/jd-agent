Course Catalog:
Tradeoffs are inherent in job choices.  Careers can either be a string of disconnected jobs or an architected endeavor resulting in both contribution to the world and personal success.  In this seminar we explore principles, frameworks, and tactics for executing a career deliberately.

Course Objective:
Everyone was put on the planet to contribute something unique and important.  An ideal professional life well lived involves discovering that unique contribution and wrapping it in a career, such that we can be both successful and true to our calling.  The objective of this course is to wrestle with the question of purpose, and to put that wrestle into the context of a career.  We ask, “what personal career trajectory would provide the best opportunity for me to both contribute to the world and enjoy professional success?”

This course provides frameworks and guides for pursuing this objective.  Think of this as more of a workshop than a class.  Much of your work will happen outside of class.  Some of it will be best pursued with a trusted advisor.  All of it will be highly personal to you and your own understanding of self.  The course is designed to teach students how to assess their current career position, chart the general direction of where they would like to go, and iteratively execute toward that objective.  The tools and frameworks taught in this class are repeatable throughout each student’s career.

If we are successful, you will gain confidence.  Confidence you can see the forest (career) for the trees (jobs).  Confidence you can make decisions that are directionally correct and contribute to the overall picture of your career.  This course will not resolve all your questions.  It will not decide what next job you should take.  But we do hope this semester of introspection and deliberate career architecting will give you a blueprint for making decisions across the arc of your career.  We hope your overall purpose will become clearer, and we hope you will gain confidence that the individual decisions you make are adding up to one great whole--a career you can be proud of

 

Overall Course Framework:
As we progress through the course, we will develop personal concepts and toolsets that will be useful the rest of our careers:

  • North Star:  this is a description of our personal objectives.  It is directional, not terminal.  We will describe these objectives such that they are helpful and motivating through career ups and downs, successes and failures, pivots and pauses.
  • The ACE process:  In this course we will do a full Assessment of who we are and where we would like to go, we will go through charting exercises that help us understand options for moving toward our North Star, and we will work on executing against those options--primarily by prototyping and “trying them on,” but eventually by actually pursuing them in the real world.  The ACE process can come in handy every few years as we experience more, learn, and adjust
  • Y Axis:  Your own personal Y axis is a consolidated representation of the things we would like to accomplish and have included in our North Star.  Everyone’s Y Axis is different.
  • TBD:  Tribe, Brand, and Domain are the lenses through which we will examine every objective, every option, and every decision.  They will keep us true to ourselves and ensure that we are working with the right people, working on the right things, and mastering the right skills and abilities that allow us to contribute at a high level throughout our careers.

Assessment
We will assess your participation and contribution the same way we assess executives in the real world--you are expected to be a net producer (not a net consumer), and you come to class every session prepared to advance our collective understanding of the subject at hand.

Participation                                                  100%

This class is pass/fail; the point of this class is not to earn a grade.  You will get out of this class what you put into it, and you are investing in yourself.  If you turn in the assignments, you will pass.  But I highly recommend investing real emotional and intellectual energy into the exercises.  They are designed to help you get to know your present and future self better, and help you design a fulfilling life where you develop the relationships, make the contributions, and achieve the mastery important to you.
